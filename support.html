Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Bulk Page Cloner for Confluence?

Getting started is easy! Visit our comprehensive documentation for step-by-step installation and usage guides. You can install the app directly from the Atlassian Marketplace.

What information should I include in my support request?

Please include: your product version, steps to reproduce the issue, expected vs. actual behavior, screenshots if applicable, and your system information (browser, OS, etc.).

Do you offer phone support?

Currently, we provide support primarily through email at support@datapingo.com. For enterprise customers, phone support may be available upon request.

How can I request a new feature or enhancement?

We love hearing your ideas! Send feature requests to support@datapingo.com with the subject line "Feature Request" and we'll review it for future releases.

Is there a knowledge base or documentation available?

Yes! Visit our Documentation page for detailed guides, tutorials, and troubleshooting tips.
