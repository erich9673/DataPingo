About DataPingo

DataPingo was created to connect applications and make business work better. We understand that in today's fast-paced business environment, seamless integration between your tools and systems is crucial for success. Our platform bridges the gap between disparate applications, enabling streamlined workflows, improved efficiency, and better decision-making across your organization.

We specialize in building productivity apps for Atlassian Confluence and Jira — tools that millions of teams rely on every day. Our flagship product, Bulk Page Cloner for Confluence, allows teams to instantly create multiple Confluence pages from an existing page template, saving hours of repetitive manual work. Whether you're onboarding new clients, setting up project spaces, or managing recurring documentation cycles, DataPingo tools are built to handle the heavy lifting so your team can focus on what matters most.

Founded with the belief that great software should be simple and powerful, DataPingo is committed to continuous improvement and listening to our customers. Every feature we ship is informed by real-world feedback from teams who use our products daily. We take pride in being responsive, reliable, and always working toward making your workflows faster and more efficient.
