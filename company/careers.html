Join Us

Small team.
Big impact.

We're a lean group building tools that Atlassian teams use every day. If you want ownership, direct impact, and zero layers of approval — read on.

Day to day

What working here actually looks like.

No hype. Just what to expect.

End-to-end ownership

You design, build, ship, and support. There's no hand-off chain. You'll know every user of every feature you build.

Remote & async by default

Work from anywhere. We optimize for deep work, not meetings. Most of our coordination happens in writing.

Real customers, real feedback

Our apps are used by Atlassian teams globally. You'll hear directly from the people you're building for — often within days of shipping.

Fast cycles, no bureaucracy

Ideas don't sit in a backlog for a quarter. If you spot something worth fixing, you fix it. We move quickly and ship constantly.

What we believe

Our values, plainly stated.

Customer first

Every decision starts with: does this help our customers do their jobs better? We build for real people solving real problems in Jira and Confluence. If a feature doesn't make their day easier, it doesn't ship.

Quality over speed

We'd rather ship something great than ship something fast. We take the time to get things right — not because we're slow, but because half-baked solutions create more work for everyone.

Transparency

Open communication, internally and with customers. We share what we're building, why we're building it, and we welcome honest feedback — even when it's uncomfortable.

Open roles

We often hire people we know before posting a role.

No open positions right now

We're not currently hiring, but we're always interested in meeting talented people passionate about the Atlassian ecosystem. If that's you, send us an email — you might be exactly who we're looking for when the time comes.
