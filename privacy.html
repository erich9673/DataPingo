1. Information We Collect

DataPingo and our applications collect and process the following information:

  • Google Account Information: We access your Google account to read Google Sheets data that you explicitly authorize.
  • Workspace Integration Data: We send notifications to your designated communication channels (Slack, Teams, etc.) via authorized connections.
  • Spreadsheet Data: We monitor changes in the specific cells and ranges you configure across our applications.
  • Usage Data: Basic information about monitoring jobs, frequency settings, and application usage patterns.

2. How We Use Your Information

DataPingo uses your information to:

  • Monitor your specified Google Sheets for changes across all our applications
  • Send notifications to your designated communication channels (Slack, Teams, etc.)
  • Maintain active monitoring sessions and service reliability
  • Provide technical support and customer assistance
  • Improve our products and develop new features
  • Ensure security and prevent unauthorized access

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Temporary Storage: We temporarily cache spreadsheet data only to detect changes
  • No Permanent Storage: We do not permanently store your spreadsheet content
  • Secure Transmission: All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • OAuth Security: We use Google's OAuth 2.0 for secure, revocable access

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights or safety
  • With your explicit consent

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke Google Sheets access at any time via Google Account settings
  • Stop monitoring by removing the app or stopping monitoring jobs
  • Request deletion of any stored data
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

6. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with:

  • Google Sheets API: Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Slack API: Subject to Slack's Privacy Policy

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@datapingo.com
  • Website: DataPingo
