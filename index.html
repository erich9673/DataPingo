About DataPingo

DataPingo builds productivity apps for teams that live inside Atlassian tools. We started with a simple observation: the most painful parts of working in Confluence aren't the hard problems — they're the repetitive ones. Creating the same page structure for every new project. Copying documentation frameworks for every new client. Duplicating sprint templates for every new sprint. These tasks are mechanical, time-consuming, and error-prone when done manually.

Our apps are designed to automate those repetitive workflows so your team can focus on the work that actually requires human creativity and judgment. Bulk Page Cloner for Confluence is our first published product on the Atlassian Marketplace, and it's just the beginning. We're actively developing new apps to cover more of the workflows that slow Confluence and Jira users down every day.

All DataPingo apps are built on Atlassian Forge, meaning they run securely in Atlassian's cloud infrastructure. Your data never leaves Atlassian's environment, so you can maintain compliance and data sovereignty even as you automate more of your workflows.
