Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my Google Sheets?

Simply authorize DataPingo to access your Google Sheets through our secure OAuth integration. No API keys or complex setup required.

Can I customize alert messages?

Yes! You can fully customize alert messages, including dynamic data from your sheets and custom formatting for different channels.

What's the alert frequency?

DataPingo checks for changes every 1-5 minutes depending on your plan. Enterprise customers can get real-time alerts.
