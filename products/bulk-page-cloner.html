Highlights

Cloning without the busywork

The parts teams quietly dread — rebuilding the same page again and again — collapse into a single pass.

In one pass

One page becomes twenty-five

Point at a page you already trust and spin off as many as 25 named copies in a single run. The work that used to eat an afternoon — this quarter's reports, each sprint's board, every new hire's runbook — is done before your coffee cools.

True to the original

Every copy is the real thing

Land them anywhere
