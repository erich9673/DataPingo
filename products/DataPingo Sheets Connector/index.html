How DataPingo Sheets Connector Works

Track Spreadsheet Updates
Monitor specific cells, rows, or entire Google Sheets for live data changes. Never miss a critical update.
Instant Slack Alerts
Send customizable notifications directly to Slack channels. Instantly alert your team the moment data changes!
No-Code Setup

How To Use DataPingo Sheets Connector

1

Login to the Application

Start by logging into the DataPingo application with your credentials. The login process is straightforward and secure.

2

Application Overview

Here's your complete application dashboard at a glance. You can see all your monitoring jobs, settings, and quick access to key features.

3

Connect Your Google Account

First, connect to your Google Account to access your Google Sheets. This secure connection allows DataPingo to monitor your spreadsheets.

4

Configure Monitoring Conditions

Select your spreadsheet, choose the specific sheet, and set your monitoring conditions. Press "Test" to validate your conditions before activating.

5

Set Up Slack Notifications

Add your Slack webhook URL to receive notifications when your Google Sheets data changes. Don't know how to get it? We provide step-by-step instructions.

6

Monitor Active Jobs

Keep track of all your active monitoring jobs in this section. You can pause individual jobs or stop all monitoring activities as needed.

7

Receive Real-Time Alerts

This is what you'll receive in Slack when your Google Sheets data changes. Clear, informative notifications that keep your team in the loop.
