Features

Everything your team needs inside Jira

No context switching. No external tools. Handoff for Jira lives entirely inside your Jira Cloud workspace.

Personal Work Queue

Build your own prioritized list of active Jira tickets across any project. Drag rows to reorder. Unlimited tickets — no caps, no subscription tiers.

Structured Ticket Handoffs

Transfer ownership with a note — search any Jira ticket, pick recipients, add context, and hit Hand off. A permanent ADF comment is auto-posted on every ticket so the history lives on the issue.

Team View

See every team member's queue side by side. Managers can instantly spot who is overloaded and check ticket priorities — without asking anyone for a status update.

Forge-Hosted — Zero External Servers

Built entirely on Atlassian Forge. All data stays inside Atlassian's infrastructure — no external API calls, no third-party storage, no data leaving your tenant.
