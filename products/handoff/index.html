How It Works

Locate Handoff for Jira under Your Apps on the left side of Jira. Click on it to open the app and dive straight into My Queue.

1

My Queue

›

Create your personal, prioritized list of active Jira tickets across any project. Drag rows to reorder.

Add tickets directly from the ••• menu on any Jira issue, or search directly in the queue bar at the top of My Queue — they land in your queue instantly.

2

Handoffs

›

Send structured handoffs from the Handoff tab — search any Jira ticket, pick recipients, add a note, and hit Hand off. Recipients get a notification in their My Queue and can accept tickets straight into their own queue. A formatted comment is auto-posted on each ticket.

3

Team View

›

See every team member's queue side by side. Managers can instantly spot who's overloaded and check ticket priorities — all without leaving Jira.
