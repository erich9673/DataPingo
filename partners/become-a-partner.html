Who this is for

Three kinds of partners we work with.

Atlassian Solution Partners

If you're an Atlassian Solution Partner, you're already talking to teams who need our apps. We make that conversation easy with joint materials and co-sell support.

Consultants & Agencies

Helping teams adopt Confluence? Our app solves the exact workflows your clients ask about — recurring pages, template rollouts, and bulk page operations.

Marketplace Resellers

Looking to bundle Atlassian apps for your customers? We're open to reseller and distribution partnerships for teams at scale.
